THE HISTORY

I'm an east-coaster with a love for fall weather and long road trips. My career has led me from my small hometown in PA, where I designed local sports programs — to Rochester, NY, where I completed my bachelors in Design and got some solid freelance work under my belt. From then on, it was all about making it in the real world — I scored an amazing job with an addy-winning company in Washington, DC (I avoided government work, don't worry) and have honed my design skills for all things digital and experiential. I'm a strong leader and a skilled, adaptive teammate.
